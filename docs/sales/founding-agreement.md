# CrewCore — DBOA Founding Chapter Agreement
**Draft for board review | June 2026**

---

## Parties

**CrewCore** — sports officials operating platform (product and services provider)

**DBOA** (Dallas Basketball Officials Association) — founding chapter

---

## Purpose

This agreement establishes the terms under which DBOA becomes the first chapter on the CrewCore platform. As the founding chapter, DBOA participates in shaping the product, receives a locked founding rate, and provides feedback that informs the platform's development priorities.

---

## What CrewCore provides

1. **CrewCore Recruit** — recruiting and onboarding workflow platform:
   - Chapter-branded public lead capture page
   - Structured onboarding workflow (configurable steps)
   - Recruiter Command Center for chapter staff
   - Recruit status page (magic-link, per-recruit)
   - Ongoing platform updates through Slice 3+ (compliance rollup, Stripe dues, AI features)

2. **Chapter sovereignty** — DBOA retains:
   - Chapter leadership and governance
   - Dues structure and bylaws
   - Training culture and evaluation process
   - Playoff eligibility rules
   - School relationships
   - Game assignment platform (Arbiter / AssignIt — unchanged)

3. **Data isolation** — DBOA data is isolated by row-level security in the platform database. No other chapter can read DBOA records.

## Pricing — DBOA founding rate

### Base fee
**$4 per official per year** (DBOA founding rate; standard rate $5/official/year).

The base fee is a whole-roster, year-round platform fee. CrewCore walks every official — new and veteran — through onboarding and clearance each season. The charge applies to all officials on the Dec-31 roster, not only new recruits.

The $4 founding rate is **locked** for DBOA at launch and does not increase unless mutually agreed in writing.

### Success fee
**+ 10% of first-year dues, for new and transfer officials only.**

- Applies to any official in their first year with the chapter — new *or* transfer
- Calculated against the dues they actually paid during that calendar year
- Applies in the calendar year they join only; in year two they convert to the $4 base count
- December 31 cutoff: officials joining after Dec 31 roll into the following year's 10% bucket

### DBOA dues schedule (2026–27, nonrefundable)

| Member type | Dues |
|---|---|
| New | $125 |
| Returning (effective April 1) | $175 |
| Returning-from-inactive | $175 |
| Transfer | $175 |

Officials continue to register in RefTown as part of CrewCore onboarding. Dues flow through DBOA's existing registration process.

### DBOA cost estimate

| Scenario | Calculation | Total |
|---|---|---|
| Base (steady state) | ~300 officials × $4 | $1,200 / year |
| Success fee example | 60 new officials × $125 × 10% | $750 |
| Heavy recruiting year | Base + success fee | ~$1,950 |
| Lean year | Base only | ~$1,200 |

### Billing

Annual invoice, issued after December 31 based on the roster snapshot and first-year-official count for that calendar year.

### Enforcement path

Year one: invoice-based. Once Stripe dues integration ships (target: Slice 3), the 10% success fee becomes automatically attributable and collectible at the point of dues payment. No change to the rate or terms — only the collection mechanism.

## What is not included

- **CrowdIQ / managed recruiting campaigns** — AI-managed ad campaigns (CrowdIQ) are a separate service, not bundled with the base platform fee. Priced per engagement if and when used.
- **Game assignment** — CrewCore does not replace Arbiter, AssignIt, or any game assignment platform.

---

## Founding-chapter participation

As the founding chapter, DBOA agrees to:

- Provide a primary contact (chapter staff or admin) to coordinate onboarding and workflow configuration
- Provide feedback on platform features prior to wider rollout
- Allow anonymized product metrics to be used for platform improvement

In exchange, CrewCore agrees to:

- Prioritize DBOA-requested features in the roadmap (within reason and product vision)
- Provide direct support access to the product team
- Lock the $4/official founding rate for the life of the agreement unless both parties agree to a change

---

## Term and termination

- **Initial term:** one year from the date of first invoice
- **Renewal:** annual, on the same terms unless modified by written agreement
- **Termination:** either party may terminate with 60 days written notice before the next annual invoice date
