# CrewCore Recruit Brief

CrewCore Recruit is the recruiting and onboarding module inside CrewCore.

## Purpose
Recruit, capture, and convert new officials through a structured onboarding funnel.

## Core goals
- increase official recruiting pipeline
- improve onboarding conversion
- reduce drop-off
- route recruits to the correct chapter
- transition recruits into training and assignment readiness

## Primary users
- chapter recruiter
- chapter admin
- assigner
- trainer
- prospect official

## AI focus
- lead scoring
- drop-off prediction
- chapter routing recommendations
- campaign drafting
- readiness summaries
